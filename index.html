About Me

I am a full stack developer who is currently honing his skills in the Denver University Coding Bootcamp program. Throughout this program, I have strengthened my skills with client-side technologies including HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, and jQuery. In addition, I have gained skills leveraging server-side technologies including Node.js, express, handlebars, and firebase. I have a particular passion in working with, and really understanding JavaScript and Node.js. The creativity that web development allows in both Front-End and Back-End web development make for a very rewarding and interesting career.

At this time, I am working as a Medical Laboratory Scientist at the University of Colorado Hospital Anschutz Medical Campus. This work is extremely interesting and rewarding. My current work involves transfusion medicine, and the transfusion of blood products and their derivatives.

I also have my Airframe and Powerplant license with an FCC endorsement. I worked as a civilian aircraft electrician in both regional and general aviation.
